# Sprint 7 P4 - Controlled Productization Readiness

## Scope

Read-only readiness for purchases and voided documents. It evaluates gates, blockers and Portal dependencies without enabling production.

## What readiness means

- The record can be evaluated against foundation gates.
- Missing dependencies are visible.
- External approval, Content/File and Notification dependencies are explicit.

## What readiness does not mean

- No SRI production or SRI Test real.
- No official ATS.
- No legal/final RIDE.
- No productive XAdES.
- No evidence upload.
- No notification delivery.
- No automatic accounting or accounts payable.

## Gates

- Tax catalogs.
- Purchase/voided validation.
- External approval workflow foundation.
- Portal Content/File boundary.
- Portal Notification boundary.
- Portal Shell context, flags and permissions.

## Result

All readiness remains `BlockedFoundation` until Portal contracts and external legal/tax approvals are finalized.
